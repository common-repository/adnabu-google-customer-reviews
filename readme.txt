A woo-commerce google customer reviews

== Description ==
A simple, hassle free plugin to add google customer reviews code to your woocommerce website.

= Steps to Create Pixel  =
1. Click on the Google Customer Review submenu in AdNabu Menu.
2. Click on the "Create Google Customer Reviews Pixel". You will be redirected to adnabu.com.
3. Login with the email connected to merchant center account. Upon successful login you will see a message with status of creation of pixel
4. Go back to Google Customer Reviews Home configure the setting of Opt-In, Badge and  Product reviews.
5. You can re-configure and manage the Google Customer Reviews settings at the App's Home

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What does this app do? =
The app adds the google customer review code to your woocommerce shop thus enabling you to
1. collect user reviews for store
2. get a google rating badge for your store
3. collect product reviews

= I enabled the Google Rating Badge but it is not showing on the store? =
In most cases, you must have at least 100 reviews in a particular country during the
past year for your seller rating to appear on your badge.

= Why I need to configure average delivery days? =
Google only ask for consent in the opt in survey.
It waits for a few days to make sure that the customer have used the product.
The review mail is not sent before expected delivery.

= Why I need to add the GTIN column for Product Reviews?=
GTIN (Global Trade Item Number) is used by google to uniquely identify a product. If you wish to collect product views you must have the gtin information in the product


= Where can I get my products GTIN numbers? =
GTIN numbers are generally the barcode on the product.
for more info visit [here]( https://www.gtin.info/)
